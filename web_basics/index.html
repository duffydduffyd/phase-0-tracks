Become a Food Feedback Pro Coach

Food Feedback is a mobile app that connects people who want to share the beautiful meals they create with food coaches they choose.

We are looking for quality and qualified people to join our coaching community.

Here are some specific things you need:

  1. You need a qualification.

  3. You need to be a US Resident
  • Passion about eating healthy
  • In love with encouragine people to make better choices
  • Understanding and respect of the culture of food specific to the community our Food Feedbackers come from
